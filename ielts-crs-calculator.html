How Language Affects Skill Transferability Bonus Points

CLB 9+ in your first language doesn't just add language points — it also unlocks additional Skill Transferability bonus points of up to 50 extra CRS points:

  • CLB 9+ + post-secondary degree: up to 25 additional CRS points
  • CLB 9+ + 3+ years Canadian work experience: up to 25 additional CRS points
  • CLB 9+ + 3+ years foreign work experience: up to 25 additional CRS points
📊

Reaching CLB 9 (IELTS 7.0) could add up to 50 + 56 = 106 total CRS points when counting both the direct language increase and the newly unlocked skill transferability bonuses. Calculate your full CRS score to see your complete picture.

IELTS to CRS — Frequently Asked Questions

IELTS GT 7.0 in all four skills converts to: Listening 7.0 = CLB 9 (31 pts), Reading 7.0 = CLB 9 (31 pts), Writing 7.0 = CLB 9 (31 pts), Speaking 7.0 = CLB 9 (31 pts). Total: 124 CRS language points (first language, no spouse). If you have a spouse, it's 116 pts (29 pts × 4).
IELTS GT Listening 8.5 = CLB 10 = 34 CRS points for that skill (first language, no spouse). This is the maximum possible for any single skill. Note: Listening 8.5 is CLB 10, but Listening 8.0 is also CLB 10 since the threshold is 8.5 only for CLB 10+. Listening 7.5 = CLB 8 = 23 pts. Listening 8.0 = CLB 10 = 34 pts.
The minimum IELTS GT scores for Express Entry programs are:
• FSW (Federal Skilled Worker): CLB 7 = L:6.0, R:6.0, W:6.0, S:6.0
• CEC (TEER 0/1): CLB 7 = L:6.0, R:6.0, W:6.0, S:6.0
• CEC (TEER 2/3): CLB 5 = L:5.0, R:4.0, W:5.0, S:5.0
• FST (Federal Skilled Trades): CLB 5 reading/writing, CLB 4 listening/speaking
These are minimums to enter the pool, but competitive scores are CLB 9+ (IELTS 7.0) for most draws.
No. IRCC only accepts IELTS General Training for Express Entry immigration applications. IELTS Academic is not accepted. This is one of the most common mistakes applicants make. If you've already taken IELTS Academic, you must retake the General Training version for your Express Entry application. Visit IELTS.org to register for IELTS GT.
CLB 7 → CLB 9 (IELTS 6.0 → 7.0 all skills): +56 CRS points (68→124)
CLB 8 → CLB 9 (IELTS 6.5 → 7.0): +32 CRS points (92→124)
CLB 9 → CLB 10 (IELTS 7.0 → 8.0): +12 CRS points (124→136)
Plus, CLB 9+ unlocks Skill Transferability bonuses worth up to 50 additional CRS points. The jump from CLB 7 to CLB 9 is the most impactful single improvement you can make to your CRS score.
French language proficiency adds CRS points in two ways: (1) Second language points: If French is your second language (English being first), scoring CLB 9+ in French earns 6 pts per skill × 4 = 24 pts. (2) French proficiency bonus: Scoring NCLC 7+ in all French skills AND CLB 5+ in English earns a +25 CRS point bonus. If you also have strong English (CLB 5+), this becomes a +50 CRS point bonus. This bonus makes French speakers competitive even with lower overall CRS scores. See IRCC's Express Entry French proficiency page.
