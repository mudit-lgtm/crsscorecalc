Who We Are

CRS Score Calculator is a free online tool designed to help Canadian immigration applicants accurately calculate their Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) score for Canada's Express Entry immigration system. Our platform is built by immigration enthusiasts and developers who understand the complexity and stress of the immigration journey.

We are not a licensed immigration consultant or law firm. We are an independent informational resource dedicated to making the CRS scoring process transparent, simple, and accessible to everyone — regardless of budget or location.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: make Canada's Express Entry CRS score calculation free, accurate, and easy to understand for every applicant around the world.

We believe that immigration information should not be locked behind expensive consultancy fees. Every person deserves to know their CRS score before spending thousands of dollars on the actual immigration process.

What We Offer
  • CRS Calculator – Full Comprehensive Ranking System score calculator based on official IRCC criteria
  • Express Entry Calculator – Specific tool for general Express Entry pool scoring
  • CEC Calculator – Canadian Experience Class specific scoring tool
  • FSW Calculator – Federal Skilled Worker program calculator
  • PNP Calculator – Provincial Nominee Program scoring
  • IELTS to CRS Converter – Convert your IELTS band scores to CRS points instantly
  • Provincial Calculators – Ontario, Alberta, BC, Manitoba, Nova Scotia, Healthcare & STEM
  • Express Entry Draws Tracker – Latest draw results with minimum CRS cutoff history
  • CRS Cutoff Score History – Historical cutoff trends to plan your Express Entry strategy
  • Immigration Blog – Practical guides on improving your CRS score, language tests, education points, and more
Accuracy & Disclaimer

Our calculators are built based on the official IRCC Comprehensive Ranking System grid. We update our scoring formulas whenever IRCC announces changes to ensure you always get the most accurate estimate.

However, please note that this tool provides an estimate only. Final CRS scores are calculated by IRCC when you create your official Express Entry profile. We strongly recommend verifying your score through your official IRCC account or by consulting a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC).
